Quiz Competition Policies

General Policies

  • All participants must register prior to the competition.
  • The competition is open to all age group.
  • Only one participant is allowed per registration.
  • The competition will start on time and late participants may not be allowed to join.

Quiz Policies

  • Questions will be read aloud by the quizmaster and participants will have a limited time to answer.
  • Participants are not allowed to use any electronic devices during the quiz.
  • Participants are not allowed to speak or communicate with others during the quiz.
  • Participants have to choose the correct option from the provided MCQ's.
  • Once the quiz has started, participants may not leave the room until the quiz is over.

Scoring Policies

  • Answers will be scored by the quizmaster and the judges.
  • The scoring is final and there will be no appeals.
  • The participant with the highest score at the end of the quiz will be declared the winner.

Agreement

By registering for the quiz competition, participants agree to the following:

  • To abide by the rules and regulations of the competition.
  • To allow the use of their name and image for promotional purposes.
  • To release the organizers and sponsors from any liability or responsibility in case of injury, loss, or damage.
